Dominik Christian Maier

Dominik Maier, B.Sc. is currently researching a novel mobile authentication method at Politecnico di Milano (Italy).

The results will be included in his Thesis of the Master's program he persues in Computer Science at University of Erlangen-Nuremberg (Germany).

2014 to 2015 he examined the security of device firmware on a large scale at SecLab UC Santa Barbara (CA, USA).
